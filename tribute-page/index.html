Nikola Tesla

List of Tesla's Achievements

  • 1891 - Invented the Tesla coil, an induction coil, widely used today in radio and television sets and other electronic equipment.
  • 1893 - Westinghouse used Tesla’s alternating current system to light the World’s Columbian Exposition at Chicago.
  • 1895 - Experimented with shadowgraphs that later were to be used by Wilhelm Röntgen when he discovered X-rays.
  • 1898 - Announced his invention of a teleautomatic boat guided by remote control. When skepticism was voiced, Tesla proved his claims for it before a crowd in Madison Square Garden.
  • 1899 - Discovered terrestrial stationary waves.He proved that Earth could be used as a conductor and made to resonate at a certain electrical frequency.

Three Nobel Prize recipients addressed their tribute to “one of the outstanding intellects of the world who paved the way for many of the technological developments of modern times.
